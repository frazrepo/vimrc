INTRODUCTION                                    *fz-macro*

Apply macro to all lines
>
    :%norm! @a
<
Apply macro to range of lines
>
    :5,10norm! @a
    :5,$norm! @a
<
Apply macro to a pattern of lines
>
    :g/pattern/normal! @a
<
Apply macro to visual selection of lines
>
    :'<,'>norm! @a
<
