INTRODUCTION                                    *fz-regexp*

Delete any empty lines
>
    :g/^$/ d
<
Reduce multiples blank lines to a single blank
>
    :g/^$/,/./-j
<
Reverse the order of the lines starting from the line 10 up to the line 20
>
    :10,20g/^/ mo 10
<
Delete all lines not matching a pattern
>
    :g!/foo/d  or :v/foo/d
<
Find this or that
>
    /this\|that
<
Find this and that
>
    /.*this\&.*that
<
Change whole word foo to bar
>
    :%s/\<foo\>/bar/gc
<
